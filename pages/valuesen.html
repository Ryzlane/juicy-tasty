At Juicy & Tasty, we place great importance on respecting some of our values

Vegan

At Juicy & Tasty, we have values that we value very much. The vegan aspect is our main, so we are committed to offer you menus that you can order with your eyes closed: if you are a seasoned vegan, nothing will contradict your convictions!

Anti-waste

We joined Too Good To Go, an application that avoids food waste. Thus, the unsold ones are recovered to avoid spoiling the least food! If you are interested, you can visit the website here !

Home baked

We also like something else: the homemade. At Juicy & Tasty, we do everything ourselves and this before your eyes! Every morning we simply cut the ingredients necessary for our dishes and then make the preparation at your order, ensuring a freshness without equal!
